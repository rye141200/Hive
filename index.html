Select Mode

Objective: Surround your opponent's Queen Bee piece completely while ensuring your own Queen Bee is not fully surrounded.

Game Setup: Each player starts with 11 pieces: 1 Queen Bee, 2 Spiders, 2 Beetles, 3 Grasshoppers, and 3 Soldier Ants.

Placing the First Piece: The first piece placed by each player must touch no other pieces. Subsequent placements must only touch pieces of the same color.

Queen Bee Placement: The Queen Bee must be placed within the first four turns.

Moving Pieces: Pieces can move around the Hive according to their unique movement rules. A piece may not break the Hive into separate groups during its move.

Spider Movement: Spiders move exactly three spaces around the edge of the Hive.

Beetle Movement: Beetles move one space at a time and can climb on top of other pieces, blocking them.

Grasshopper Movement: Grasshoppers jump in a straight line over one or more pieces to the next empty space.

Soldier Ant Movement: Soldier Ants can move any number of spaces around the edge of the Hive.

Queen Bee Movement: The Queen Bee can move one space at a time around the edge of the Hive.

Breaking the Hive Rule: No piece can be moved in a way that splits the Hive into separate groups.

Winning the Game: The game is won when a player surrounds their opponent's Queen Bee completely (with pieces of any color).
